Types Of Production In India

There are many kinds of Steel Products Across India.

Flat Products

SAIL(Bokaro Steel Plant) produces flat product like sheets, slabs ,coils etc.

Long Products

SAIL(Bhilai Steel Plant) produces Long product like tmts,rails etc.

Long & Finished Productions

SAIL(Durgapur Steel Plant) produces tmts, semi finished steel products etc.

Advanced Flat Products

SAIL(Rourkela Steel Plant) produces flat product like HR plates ,HR coils SW pipes ERW pipes etc.
